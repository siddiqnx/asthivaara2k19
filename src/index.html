About Our College

Easwari Engineering College, a unit of SRM Group of Educational Institutions for higher learning is functioning under “Valliammai Society”. The society was founded in the year 1981, for promoting the cause of Quality Education by Dr.T.R.Pachamuthu, an academician and Educationist. EEC was instituted in the academic year 1996-1997, with the Approval of AICTE, New Delhi. The Institution is affiliated to Anna University, since 2002. The college has obtained ISO 9001:2008 Certiﬁcation from TUV South Asia. The college is accredited with ‘A’ Grade by NAAC.

About Asthivaara 2K19

The Department of Civil Engineering has been established in the College since the year 2007-2008 with an annual intake of 60 students. The intake is increased to 120 from the academic year 2012-2013. PG Structural Engineering started in the year of 2013-14 with the intake of 18. The department is providing basic and advanced knowledge, inspiring and awakening young minds by vibrant teachers. The program is accredited by NBA.
